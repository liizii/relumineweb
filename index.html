Relumine is a puzzle adventure game. A child named Relu delves into an abandoned mine and discovers a magic crystal. With the crystal’s power to rotate the world, she explores the ruins of an ancient society, is chased by a giant shadow and guided by mysterious spirits, in search of her long lost Papa.






Highlights


♦ Unique Rubik's Cube like mechanic and level design that blends gameplay with storytelling, allowing the player to learn about the world while they play.

♦ Discover magic and unravel the mysteries of the unknown, as Relumine presents an alternate perspective of the world, as seen through a child’s eyes.

About

We encourage you to challenge your own perceptions, and in doing so you may discover a bit of magic as you enjoy Relumine.
